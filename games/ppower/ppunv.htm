Foreword

To put all the bla-bla in few words : Pure Power is not a game that has a story, but a story that has a game. What does this means ? That our main concern was to create the universe and the atmosphere and then to make the interface for the player with this universe : the game. Moving units from here to there is just a piece in a mechanism that works to keep the player involved in the universe. Basically this is less a real time tactical game, than an interactive book. Meaning that everything - music, sounds, graphics, backgrounds, stories, historical events, characters, tactical moves - has to submit to this goal - of keeping the player here.

"Keeping the player involved" is that kind of sintagm that we hate about PR reports. For not-so-crisp-people, it means we're trying to make our players fondly remember that they've once played the role of a tac-commander and took part at an far-from-reality adventure.

So you will interact with the Pure Power universe through three games : Pure Power, PP online and Pure Power 2. These games will polish every aspect of the PP universe until you will be ready to face the AR universe. But those are just a glimpses of the future - promises of the days to come.

The Diary of a Young Warrior
yr. 1999, tacship "Eris" digital archives

05.12.1982 UST
"My father returned home today. Oh I'm so happy to see him. Several days ago I saw on Global Net daily reports that he saved the emperor's life. The emperor wanted to inspect some thagian orbital platforms and test the new plasma cannons against the thagian fighters carcasses. He only wanted one wing man, and that was my father. They approached the platform, and were suddenly jumped by 6 thagian fighters. After a short dogfight, the thagian fighters went bye bye, their carcasses floating aimlessly in space. My father incinerated 4 thagians pilots, 2 of them trying to shoot the emperor's six. The following days I will train my sword skills against a honorable hero that is my father. Even if I'll lose, at least I have the excuse a honorable man beat me."

 24.02.1993 UST
"Today is a mourning day for me. Me and my tacsquad returned from a routine patrol on the sands of the border world Clearance IV, and the commander of the garrison gave me the news of my parents death. A new colony had to be established on the border world of Remainder VI. My mother commanded the ground garrison, while my father was in command of the stellar fleet; his destroyer "Hammer" and the carrier "Nova" surveyed the drop ships as they carried people and materials to the planet surface. One week later, a barbarian fleet exited from The Ocean and attacked out people. "Nova" died very soon in a ball of flames, while my father's "Hammer" still fought desperately to allow the civilians to escape. The fight took 12 hours, and the dishonorable barbarians finally used interdicted nuclear missiles to wipe out the civilians and garrison on the planet. "Hammer" was hitted hard by the nuclear missiles, and eventually became a mini sun. This was the end of my parents and of more than 10000 people, both military and civilians. Several days later, a clan Steel Iris cruiser arrived in Remainder system, alerted by the escaping civilians. There was nothing more to do for our people, but the cruiser managed to identify the attackers. They were the barbarians known as (?). I was informed of this, and now I know the one who will taste my wraith when my tac-commander skills will grow strong enough."

17.05.1993 UST
"My stage on Clearance IV finished today and I'm ready to leave the Clearance and take the command of a tac-squad. I headed for the star port and I was surprised to see a huge cruiser of my clan standing there.As I approached, I recognized the cruiser as "Independence". One moment later, I saw the smiling face of Adar Sum, my father best friend. Then I find myself running madly toward him, and he give me a big hug. He told me that I'm under his custody now, and he will look upon my training from now on. He was to take me in a tour of the Core and Border Worlds, so that I'll meet people and see places and my tac-commander training will be thus completed."


20.12.1998 UST
"Independence drop me today on my home world. I had completed my training. I lead a small tac-squad in several battles, I saw the incredible Eritrean Worlds, I saw the star ship construction sites of Port Said, the clans' armories, I met the clan leaders and trained with the clans weapons masters. I made the honorable thagians warriors feel the power of my sword. I delivered pure pain to the unworthy barbarian. I am now ready for the war to come against Thage."

27.12.1998 UST
"A meet with the emperor could mean two things: death or glory. Today I received a new message in the comm room, and guess what ? In the red glow of the holoprojector, stood the emperor himself. I bow before him, and he told me to raise. He told me that the war with Thage will start in several weeks, as agreed on a meeting on Allegiance V with thagian leaders, and they will have the offensive opportunity this time. He also told me that talented young tac-commanders are needed in this war, to show that The Interface designed 50 years ago hasn't aged and is still operational and ready to aid The Rion win new victories on the battlefield. In the memory of my father, he granted me access to clan armories, to clan garrisons and to the spectre clusters, so that I will have the best trained men and the latest military hardware. Also, I was granted with one of the fastest troop carrier designed in the space docks of Port Said. Now I'm waiting my tacsquad and the "Eris", the ship that will help me sail through The Ocean to reach battle sites. Until then, my blade stays eager."
